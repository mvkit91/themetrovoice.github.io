Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2024

Introduction

At TheMetroVoice, we are committed to protecting your privacy while providing our innovative Talk-To-The-Site! Chrome extension. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our service.

Data Processing

Temporary Data Processing

When you use Talk-To-The-Site!, the content of the webpage you're interacting with and your queries are temporarily processed by our Language Learning Model (LLM) server to generate responses.

No Data Storage

We do not store any of your queries, the webpage content, or the generated responses. All data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your browsing habits.

How Our Extension Works

1. When you interact with a webpage through Talk-To-The-Site!:

  • The webpage content is sent to our LLM server
  • Your query is processed alongside the webpage content
  • A response is generated and sent back to your browser
  • All data is immediately discarded after processing

2. We use secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls to processing systems
  • Immediate data disposal after processing
